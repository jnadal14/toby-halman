About

Toby Halman is a graphic designer based in Toronto, Ontario, with five years of agency experience crafting brands and visual systems that connect with clients and end users.

With a background in Industrial Design from OCAD University, he approaches branding through both strategic thinking and creative execution. His experience spans the nightlife, restaurant, real estate, and cannabis sectors, where he has helped businesses develop distinctive identities and cohesive brand experiences.
